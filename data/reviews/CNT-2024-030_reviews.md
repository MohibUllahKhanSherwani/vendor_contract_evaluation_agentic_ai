# Contract Review - CNT-2024-030
## Sahara Oilfield Chemicals - Quarterly Performance Check

### Review Period
**Q1-Q2 2024** (January - June 2024)  
**Reviewer**: Amna Al-Balushi, Production Operations  
**Review Date**: July 15, 2024  
**Contract Status**: UNDER MONITORING

---

## Overall Assessment: **BORDERLINE PERFORMANCE**

Sahara Chemicals (SC) has provided Daleel with production chemicals for over 18 months. Their technical expertise is high, however, their logistical execution has become increasingly unreliable. 

## Concerns
1. **OTD Performance**: Delivery accuracy has dropped below 90% in May. This is unacceptable for just-in-time operations.
2. **Technical Support**: Analysis of field samples (lab turnaround) is now averaging 7.2 days, far exceeding our 5-day requirement. 
3. **Quality Control**: The Batch-12B contamination incident was a significant operational risk. 

## Recommendation
**MONITORING** status. SC must demonstrate improved OTD and lab efficiency by the end of Q3. If performance does not recover to 95%+ OTD, we will begin looking for a secondary supplier for the South Block.
