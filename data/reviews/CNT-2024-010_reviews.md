# Contract Review - CNT-2024-010
## Desert Drilling Solutions - Mid-Year Evaluation

### Review Period
**Q1-Q2 2024** (January - June 2024)  
**Reviewer**: Khalid Al-Mashani, Drilling Manager  
**Review Date**: July 10, 2024  
**Contract Status**: RENEGOTIATION RECOMMENDED

---

## Overall Assessment: **SAFE BUT INEFFICIENT**

Desert Drilling Solutions (DDS) maintains an exemplary safety record, which is critical in our operations. However, their mechanical performance and drilling efficiency have notably declined in Q2. NPT is currently at 11.2%, significantly above the 8% target and the 12% industry baseline.

---

## Highlights
- **HSE Excellence**: Zero Lost Time Incidents (LTI) in Q1/Q2. Outstanding near-miss reporting culture.
- **Crisis Response**: The response to the April sensor failure was textbook, preventing a major incident.

## Major Concerns
- **Equipment Reliability**: Rig uptime dropped below 90% in April due to recurring top-drive issues.
- **Cost Management**: 6% budget overrun driven by reactionary maintenance costs.

## Recommendation
Proceed with **RENEGOTIATION**. We need a contractual commitment to a structured Preventive Maintenance (PM) program and potentially an NPT-based penalty/bonus scheme to align incentives.
