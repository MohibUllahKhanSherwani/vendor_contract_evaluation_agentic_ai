# Pipeline & Integrity - Industry Benchmarks (2024)

## Performance Standards

### Inspection Coverage (ILI/Manual)
- **Target**: > 98% of planned pipeline length inspected annually
- **Industry Average**: 92% - 95%
- **Below Standard**: < 90%

### Defect Detection Accuracy
- **Target Rate**: > 97% accuracy compared to verification excavations
- **Warning Sign**: False negative rate (missed defects) > 5%

### Repair Response Time
- **Critical (Leak/Imminent Failure)**: < 24 hours mobilization
- **High (Significant Corrosion)**: < 7 days
- **Medium**: < 30 days

## Cost Benchmarks (2024)

### Intelligent Pigging (per km)
- **Base Rate**: $800 - $1,200
- **Specialized (High Resolution)**: $1,500 - $2,200

## HSE & Safety Benchmarks
- **Leak Frequency**: < 0.05 per 1,000 km
- **Contractor Compliance**: 100% adherence to Excavation & Ground Disturbance permits.
- **Environmental Incidents**: Zero spills > 1 barrel threshold.
