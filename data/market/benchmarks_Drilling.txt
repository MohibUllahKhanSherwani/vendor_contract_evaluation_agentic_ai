# Drilling & Well Services - Industry Benchmarks (Oman/MENA 2024)

## Performance Standards

### Non-Productive Time (NPT)
- **Industry Average**: 12% - 15% of total rig operations
- **Top Tier Providers**: < 8%
- **Below Standard**: > 18%

**2024 Trend**: NPT has slightly increased due to increased exploration in more complex geological formations and equipment age in some older fleets.

### Rig Uptime & Availability
- **Industry Average**: 92% - 95% 
- **Best-in-Class**: > 97%
- **Concerning Threshold**: < 90%

### Wellbore Quality Index
- **Target**: > 90% deviation from planned trajectory
- **Warning Sign**: Multiple sidetracks required due to avoidable drilling issues.

## Cost Benchmarks (2024)

### Rig Day Rates (Onshore)
- **Standard 1500HP Rig**: $22,000 - $28,000
- **Advanced Cyber Rigs**: $32,000 - $38,000
- **Budget/Older Fleets**: $18,000 - $22,000

## HSE & Safety Benchmarks (Drilling)
- **LTIR Target**: < 0.20 per 200k man-hours
- **TRIR Target**: < 0.80 per 200k man-hours
- **Near-Miss Frequency**: High reporting ( > 20 per month) indicates strong safety culture. low reporting ( < 5) is a red flag for hidden risks.

## Risk Assessment Context

### High Risk Indicators
- NPT consistently > 20%
- History of well control incidents or near-blowouts
- Rig uptime < 85%
- Maintenance backlog > 3 months on critical spares
