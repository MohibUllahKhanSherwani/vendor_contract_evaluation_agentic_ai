# Oilfield Chemical Supply - Industry Benchmarks (2024)

## Performance Standards

### Delivery Timeline Accuracy (JIT)
- **Target**: > 96% on-time delivery (OTD)
- **Industry Average**: 92%
- **Below Standard**: < 88%

### Product Quality Consistency
- **Compliance**: 99.5% of batches meeting technical specifications (Batch Concentration, Purity)
- **Warning Sign**: Returns/Recalls > 2 per year

### Technical Support Response
- **On-site Engineer Availability**: < 48 hours for problem diagnosis
- **Lab Analysis Turnaround**: < 5 business days for field samples

## Cost Benchmarks (2024)

### Scale Inhibitor / Emulsifier Sets
- **Standard Formulations**: $4.50 - $6.50 per liter
- **Specialized/High Temperature**: $8.00 - $12.00 per liter

## Risk Assessment Context

### High Risk Indicators
- History of contamination in delivered chemicals
- Repeated stock-outs (empty tanks) causing production shutdowns
- Non-compliance with Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) regulations
- Long lead times > 6 weeks for critical production chemicals
